Junior Architect

Navami Housing

Overview

Navami Housing is a premium residential development in Bengaluru designed to offer contemporary urban living through thoughtfully planned apartments, lifestyle amenities, landscaped open spaces, and a dedicated clubhouse that promotes community interaction. The project focuses on efficient space planning, modern architectural design, and high-quality residential environments suitable for contemporary families.

Key Responsibilities

  • Developed architectural drawings across various design stages.
  • Contributed to the planning and layout development of the clubhouse and associated amenity spaces.
  • Created and maintained detailed 3D BIM models in Revit for architectural interiors.
  • Produced high-quality interior visualizations and presentation renders using Lumion to support design communication and client presentations.
