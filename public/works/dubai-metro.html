BIM Coordinator & Architect

Dubai Metro Blue Line

Overview

The Dubai Metro Blue Line is a landmark transportation infrastructure project being developed by the Roads and Transport Authority (RTA) as part of Dubai's long-term mobility strategy. The project comprises approximately 30 km of new metro alignment, 14 stations, and a dedicated depot, enhancing connectivity between key urban districts including Dubai Creek Harbour, Mirdif, Dubai Silicon Oasis, Academic City, and International City. The Blue Line is designed to support Dubai's future urban growth while promoting sustainable public transportation and digital infrastructure delivery.

As a BIM Coordinator and Architect within the Depot Package, I was responsible for architectural BIM delivery from the initial modelling stages through project coordination and digital handover. My responsibilities included developing and maintaining high-quality BIM models, ensuring timely submission of project deliverables, and coordinating architectural information across multiple design packages.

Beyond modelling, I played a key role in multi-disciplinary BIM coordination, collaborating closely with Architecture, Structure, MEP, and other engineering disciplines to resolve clashes, maintain model integrity, and ensure seamless integration throughout the project lifecycle. I was responsible for conducting comprehensive quality assurance and quality control (QA/QC) reviews, verifying compliance with RTA BIM standards and project requirements, monitoring model health, and supporting the project's digital delivery objectives. Through effective coordination, adherence to BIM execution processes, and proactive issue resolution, I contributed to the successful and timely delivery of project milestones while maintaining high standards of accuracy, compliance, and collaboration.

Key Responsibilities

  • Led multi-disciplinary BIM coordination across Architecture, Structure, MEP, and other engineering disciplines to ensure seamless model integration and effective design coordination.
  • Coordinated with internal design teams, consultants, and stakeholders to manage project deliverables, resolve technical issues, and maintain alignment with project schedules.
  • Facilitated coordination meetings, performed clash detection, and tracked issue resolution using BIM workflows to ensure timely project progression.
  • Developed and maintained detailed architectural BIM models for depot buildings in compliance with project specifications and BIM Execution Plans (BEP).
  • Conducted comprehensive QA/QC reviews to ensure model accuracy, consistency, data integrity, and compliance with RTA BIM standards and project requirements.
  • Managed digital delivery of BIM models and documentation, ensuring timely submissions while maintaining high-quality standards.
  • Monitored model health, naming conventions, BIM standards, and information consistency throughout the project lifecycle.
  • Collaborated with multidisciplinary teams to support design development, improve BIM workflows, and facilitate efficient information exchange across all project stakeholders.
