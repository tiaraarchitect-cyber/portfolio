Index 04 — Elevated Transit

Genoa SkyMetro

An elevated rapid transit system threading through Genoa's challenging topography, connecting hillside neighbourhoods to the city centre. The project moved from Schematic Design through to Design Development, resolving complex structural interfaces, station access strategies, and urban integration within a dense Italian context.
