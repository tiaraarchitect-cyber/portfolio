Index 04 — Elevated Transit

BIM Architect

Genoa SkyMetro

Overview

The Genoa SkyMetro is a proposed urban rapid transit project designed to improve public transportation in the city of Genoa, Italy. The project aims to enhance connectivity between the city centre and the Val Bisagno corridor through a modern metro system, supporting sustainable urban mobility and reducing traffic congestion. The development forms part of Genoa's long-term strategy to expand its public transport network and improve accessibility across key urban areas.

Key Responsibilities

  • Developed detailed architectural BIM models using Autodesk Revit during the design stage.
  • Worked in close coordination with the Spanish design team to incorporate design updates and revisions into the BIM model.
  • Produced architectural models and drawings in accordance with project requirements and BIM execution standards.
  • Coordinated with multidisciplinary teams to ensure architectural models remained aligned with the evolving design.
  • Supported the digital delivery process by preparing accurate BIM deliverables for project submissions.
