Fresher Architect

Amritsar Railway Station

Overview

The Amritsar Railway Station Redevelopment project is a proposed transformation of one of Punjab's busiest railway hubs into a modern, multimodal transit destination while celebrating the city's rich cultural and architectural heritage. The redevelopment focuses on improving passenger circulation, enhancing public spaces, integrating commercial facilities, and creating a contemporary station experience that reflects the identity of Amritsar as the gateway to the Golden Temple.

As a Fresher Architect, I contributed to the early design and planning stages of the project. My responsibilities included zoning studies, planning layouts, CAD drawing production, sketch model preparation, 3D visualization and rendering, as well as developing PowerPoint presentations to communicate design concepts, planning strategies, and project proposals to the internal design team. This role provided valuable exposure to large-scale transportation infrastructure planning and architectural presentation development.
