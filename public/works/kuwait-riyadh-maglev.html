Index 03 — High-Speed Transit

BIM Architect

Kuwait–Riyadh Maglev Line

Overview

The Kuwait–Riyadh Maglev Line is a proposed high-speed magnetic levitation rail corridor envisioned to enhance regional connectivity between Kuwait and Saudi Arabia as part of the GCC's long-term transportation strategy. The concept focuses on delivering a sustainable, high-capacity transit system capable of significantly reducing travel times while supporting economic integration and future urban development.

As a BIM Architect, I was responsible for the production of architectural BIM models and concept-stage drawings for the project. My work involved developing detailed digital models that translated the design intent into accurate BIM deliverables during the conceptual design phase. I also produced architectural drawings and presentation materials to support the project's early-stage development.

In addition to model production, I created high-quality 3D visualizations and walkthrough animations to effectively communicate the proposed architectural concepts, station environments, and passenger experience. My role was entirely focused on BIM production and visualization, ensuring that conceptual ideas were represented through clear, coordinated digital deliverables for design presentations and stakeholder reviews.
