Index 05 — National Infrastructure

BIM Architect

Etihad Rail

Overview

The Etihad Rail project is the UAE's national railway network, developed to establish a modern passenger and freight rail system connecting all seven emirates while enhancing regional connectivity, sustainable transportation, and economic growth. The project forms a key part of the UAE's long-term infrastructure vision, linking major cities, industrial zones, logistics hubs, and ports through a high-capacity rail network.

As a BIM Architect, I was part of the production team, contributing to architectural BIM deliverables across multiple project stages.

My Contribution

DCP4 – Stage 2 · Abu Dhabi
  • Produced and developed architectural BIM models in accordance with project standards and Level of Development (LOD) requirements.
  • Performed DCP4 model quality clearances, ensuring models were ready for Stage 2 submission.
  • Prepared and implemented model quality checklists to verify compliance with project BIM standards and client requirements.
  • Reviewed and resolved model quality issues related to geometry, parameters, documentation, and modelling consistency.
  • Ensured architectural models complied with the BIM Execution Plan (BEP) and project-specific modelling standards.
Tender Stage – Stage 4 · Dubai–Sharjah Package
  • Contributed to the production of architectural BIM models for the Stage 4 Tender submission.
  • Prepared and managed BOQ (Bill of Quantities) schedules directly from the BIM model to support tender documentation.
  • Ensured model data and schedules were accurate, coordinated, and aligned with project requirements for quantity extraction.
  • Coordinated with internal teams to incorporate design revisions and maintain consistency between BIM models and tender deliverables.
  • Supported the timely production of high-quality digital deliverables for the tender submission.
