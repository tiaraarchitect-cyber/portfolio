Tiara Raghuvanshi

I am an Architect and BIM Specialist with over four years of experience in delivering complex infrastructure, transportation, and residential projects. My portfolio includes metro systems, railway stations, depots, housing developments, and international transit projects, where I have contributed to design development, BIM implementation, and multidisciplinary coordination.

Passionate about combining design with technology, I leverage tools such as Autodesk Revit, Navisworks, and other BIM solutions to create efficient, accurate, and collaborative project workflows. I enjoy solving design challenges, optimizing digital processes, and transforming concepts into practical, high-quality design solutions.

Driven by continuous learning and innovation, I am committed to advancing digital delivery and sustainable design practices. My goal is to create impactful, future-ready environments while collaborating with multidisciplinary teams to deliver projects that leave a lasting positive impact.
